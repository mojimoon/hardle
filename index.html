Hardle v0.1.0
by mojimoon
Hardle is like Wordle but harder.
  • Green = correct letter, correct position
  • Yellow = correct letter, wrong position
  • Grey = letter not in word
However, you do not know which letter is green or yellow when you make a guess.
Instead, after each guess, the number of green and yellow letters is revealed.
Click any letter in submitted rows to cycle: green → yellow → grey → unmarked.
